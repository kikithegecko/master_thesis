What is Casual Interaction?

Interaction with technology is influenced by physical, mental and social factors
Wearables should enable casual interaction, not requiring the full attention over the whole interaction period

Scenario

Ambient light control in a Smart Home

Device Design

Key requirements: Enabling and encouraging Casual Interaction, availability, wearing comfort, battery life.
This lead to a wrist-worn wearable featuring touch and accelerometer input.

Interaction Design

Desired functionalities: On/Off, Dimming, Color mood change, precise color change, preset activation
Map functionalitites to interactions based on complexity and frequency of use.

evaluation and reception

Scenario and walkthrough with N students
All liked the device and the interaction, were engaged in a playful way
Slight problems with touch covering, tap activation and template gesture input
All participants rated the overall experience with the device with 4 on a scale from 1 (very bad) to 5 (very good)
